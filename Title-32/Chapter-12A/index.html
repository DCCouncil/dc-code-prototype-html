Legislative history of Law 20-168

Law 20-168, the “Protecting Pregnant Workers Fairness Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-769. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on July 14, 2014, and Sept. 23, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Oct. 23, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-458 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-168 became effective on Mar. 3, 2015.
