§ 50–301.33. Dome light and Taxicab Smart Meter System installation businesses.

No person or business shall violate or aid and abet a violation of public vehicle-for-hire laws, rules, and regulations applicable to the installation of a dome light or a Taxicab Smart-Meter System. The Commission shall have authority to determine, by rule, appropriate fines and penalties for violations of this section.
