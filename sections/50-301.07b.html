§ 50–301.07b. Reciprocal agreements.

The Mayor shall work with surrounding jurisdictions to update reciprocal agreements and shall submit a report to the Council on or before June 30, 2015, on his or her progress.

History

(Mar. 25, 1986, D.C. Law 6-97, § 8b, as added Apr. 23, 2013, D.C. Law 19-270, § 2(c), 60 DCR 1717; Mar. 10, 2015, D.C. Law 20-197, § 2(c), 61 DCR 12430.)

Prior Codifications

2001 Ed., § 50-307.02.

Effect of amendments

The 2013 amendment by D.C. Law 19-270 added this section.

The 2015 amendment by D.C. Law 20-197 rewrote the section.
