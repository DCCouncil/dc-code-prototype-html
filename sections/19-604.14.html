§ 19–604.14. Disclaimer.

A beneficiary may disclaim all or part of the beneficiary’s interest as provided by Chapter 15 of this title.

History

(Mar. 19, 2013, D.C. Law 19-230, § 2(b), 59 DCR 13606.)
