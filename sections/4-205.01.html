§ 4–205.01. Eligibility for public assistance.

(a) Public assistance may be awarded to, or on behalf of, any needy individual who is eligible for one of the categories of public assistance established by subchapter II.

(b) Notwithstanding any other provision of this subchapter, no person shall be rendered ineligible for public assistance by reason of a civil violation of § 48‑1201.

Law 20-126, the “Marijuana Possession Decriminalization Amendment Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-409. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on February 4, 2014, and March 4, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on March 31, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-305 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-126 became effective on July 17, 2014.
