§ 47–2887.16. Administrative penalty.

The Mayor may assess a civil penalty against an athlete agent not to exceed $25,000 for a violation of this part.

History

(Apr. 13, 2002, D.C. Law 14-107, § 3, 49 DCR 1193).)
