§ 47–1277. Rules.

The Mayor, in consultation with the Department of Health and ICF-IDD and employee representatives, shall issue rules to implement the provisions of this chapter.

History

(Mar. 8, 2006, D.C. Law 16-68, § 2(b), 53 DCR 47; Sept. 26, 2012, D.C. Law 19-169, § 33(b)(10), 59 DCR 5567.)

Section references

This section is referenced in § 47‑1274, § 47‑1275, § 47‑1276, and § 47‑1278.

Effect of amendments

The 2012 amendment by D.C. Law 19-169 substituted “ICF-IDD” for “ICF-MR.”

Legislative history of Law 16-68

For Law 16-68, see notes following § 47‑1270.

Legislative history of Law 19-169

See note to § 47‑1270.

Editor’s notes
