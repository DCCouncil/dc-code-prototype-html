§ 2–580. Training.

The Office of Boards and Commissions, established December 19, 2001 (Mayor’s Order 2001-189), in coordination with the Open Government Office, shall:

(1) Develop a training manual for members of public bodies; and

(2) Annually advise all members of public bodies of their responsibilities under this subchapter.
