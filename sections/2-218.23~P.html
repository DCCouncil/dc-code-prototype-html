History

(Oct. 20, 2005, D.C. Law 16-33, § 2323, 52 DCR 7503; July 18, 2008, D.C. Law 17-207, § 2(d), 55 DCR 6107; Apr. 20, 2010, D.C. Law 18-141, § 2(e), 57 DRC 1485.)
