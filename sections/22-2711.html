§ 22–2711. Procuring for third persons.

Any person who, within the District of Columbia, shall receive any money or other valuable thing for or on account of procuring and placing in the charge or custody of another person for sexual intercourse, prostitution, debauchery, or other immoral purposes any individual shall be guilty of a felony and, upon conviction, shall be punished by imprisonment for not more than 5 years and by a fine of not more than the amount set forth in § 22‑3571.01.

History

(June 25, 1910, 36 Stat. 833, ch. 404, § 7; as added Jan. 3, 1941, 54 Stat. 1226, ch. 936, § 4; May 21, 1994, D.C. Law 10-119, § 13(b), 41 DCR 1639; June 11, 2013, D.C. Law 19-317, § 229(e), 60 DCR 2064.)

Effect of amendments

The 2013 amendment by D.C. Law 19-317 substituted “not more than the amount set forth in § 22‑3571.01” for “not more than $1,000”.
