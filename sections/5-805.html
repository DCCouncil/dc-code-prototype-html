§ 5–805. Payment of penalties.

Penalties assessed against a member in conditional retirement as provided in § 5‑804, shall be treated as a debt owed to the District of Columbia government, pursuant to § 1‑629.03, and may be collected by the District of Columbia government, pursuant to § 1‑629.04, or by any other means authorized by law.

History

(Oct. 4, 2000, D.C. Law 13-160, § 506, 47 DCR 4619; Mar. 11, 2015, D.C. Law 20-236, § 3, 62 DCR 485.)

Section references

This section is referenced in § 5‑806.

Effect of amendments

The 2015 amendment by D.C. Law 20-236 rewrote this section.

Legislative history of Law 13-160

For Law 13-160, see notes following § 5‑801.

Legislative history of Law 20-236

Law 20-236, the “Firefighter Retirement While Under Disciplinary Investigation Amendment Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-890. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Nov. 18, 2014, and Dec. 2, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Jan. 6, 2015, it was assigned Act No. 20-561 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-236 became effective on Mar. 11, 2015.
