History

(Apr. 27, 2013, D.C. Law 19-293, § 2, 60 DCR 2613.)

Legislative history of Law 19-293

Law 19-293, the “District Of Columbia Flood Assistance Fund Amendment Act of 2012”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 19-938. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Dec. 4, 2012, and Dec. 18, 2012, respectively. Returned without signature from the Mayor on Feb. 4, 2013, it was assigned Act No. 19-661 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 19-293 became effective on Apr. 27, 2013.
