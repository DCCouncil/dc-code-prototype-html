Legislative history of Law 20-154

Law 20-154, the “Sustainable Solid Waste Management Amendment Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-641. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 24, 2014, and July 14, 2014, respectively. Returned without the Mayor’s signature on Sept. 19, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-423 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-154 became effective on Feb. 26, 2015.
