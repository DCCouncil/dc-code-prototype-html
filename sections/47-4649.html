§ 47–4649. Abatement of real property taxes for 4427 Hayes Street, N.E.

The real property described as Lot 120, Square 5129, and any improvements thereon, shall be exempt from the tax imposed by Chapter 8 of this title during tax years 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, and 2015; provided, that the total tax exemption provided by this section shall not exceed $140,000.

History

(Apr. 8, 2011, D.C. Law 18-370, § 722(b), 58 DCR 1008.)

Emergency legislation

For temporary (90 day) addition of § 47‑4649, see § 722(b) of Fiscal Year 2011 Supplemental Budget Support Emergency Act of 2010 (D.C. Act 18-694, January 19, 2011, 58 DCR 662).

Legislative history of Law 18-370

For history of Law 18-370, see notes under § 47‑143.

Short title

Short title: Section 721 of D.C. Law 18-370 provided that subtitle C of title VII of the act may be cited as “4427 Hayes Street, N.E., Real Property Tax Abatement Act of 2010”.
