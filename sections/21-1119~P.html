Legislative history of Law 19-169

See note to § 21‑1101.

Transfer of Functions

Functions of the Department of Public Welfare were transferred to the Director of the Department of Human Resources by Commissioner’s Order No. 69-96, dated March 7, 1969, as amended by Commissioner’s Order No. 70-83, dated March 6, 1970. The Department of Human Resources was replaced by the Department of Human Services by Reorganization Plan No. 2 of 1979, dated February 21, 1980.

Editor’s notes

Section 35 of D.C. Law 19-169 provided that no provision of the act shall impair any right or obligation existing under law.
