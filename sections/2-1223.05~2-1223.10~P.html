Legislative history of Law 16-19

Law 16-19, the “Anacostia Waterfront Corporation Board Expansion Amendment Act of 2005”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 16-25, which was referred to the Committee of Economic Development. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on May 3, 2005, and June 7, 2005, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on June 22, 2005, it was assigned Act No. 16-101 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 16-19 became effective on September 14, 2005.
