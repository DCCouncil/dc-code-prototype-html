§ 3–342.01. Definitions.

For the purposes of this subchapter, the term “Baseball Stadium” shall have the same meaning as that provided for the term “Ballpark” in § 47‑2002.05(a)(1)(A).

History

(Nov. 3, 1977, D.C. Law 2-37, § 3a, as added June 5, 2008, D.C. Law 17-169, § 2(a), 55 DCR 5183; Mar. 11, 2015, D.C. Law 20-233, § 201(a), 62 DCR 438.)

Effect of amendments

The 2015 amendment by D.C. Law 20-233 would have rewritten this section.

Emergency notes

For temporary (90 days) amendment of this section, see § 201(a) of the Soccer Stadium Development Emergency Amendment Act of 2014 (D.C. Act 20-557, Dec. 30, 2014, 62 DCR 448, 20 STAT 4467).

Legislative history of Law 17-169

Law 17-169, the “Ballpark Public Safety Amendment Act of 2008”, was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 17-584 which was referred to the Committee on Public Safety and Judiciary. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on March 4, 2008, and April 1, 2008, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on April 14, 2008, it was assigned Act No. 17-343 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 17-169 became effective on June 5, 2008.

Legislative history of Law 20-233

Law 20-233, the “Soccer Stadium Development Amendment Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-805. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Dec. 2, 2014, and Dec. 17, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Dec. 30, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-556 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-233 became effective on Mar. 11, 2015.

Editor’s notes

Applicability of D.C. Law 20-233: Section 301 of D.C. Law 20-233 provided (1) that the act shall apply upon the date of inclusions of its fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan; (2) that the Chief Financial Officer shall certify the date of the inclusion of the fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan, and provide notice to the Budget Director of the Council of the certification; and (3) that the Budget Director shall cause the notice of the certification to be published in the District of Columbia Register and that the date of publication of the notice of the certification shall not affect the applicability of the act.
