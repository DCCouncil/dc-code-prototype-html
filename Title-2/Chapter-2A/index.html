Legislative history of Law 20-228

Law 20-228, the “Public-Private Partnership Act of 2014,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-595. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on Oct. 28, 2014, and Dec. 2, 2014, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on Dec. 29, 2014, it was assigned Act No. 20-550 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-228 became effective on Mar. 11, 2015.

Subchapter V. Applicability; Construction.

§ 2–275.01. Applicability; construction.

(a) (1) This chapter shall apply upon the date of inclusion of its fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan.

(2) The Chief Financial Officer shall certify the date of the inclusion of the fiscal effect in an approved budget and financial plan, and provide notice to the Budget Director of the Council of the certification.

(3) (A) The Budget Director shall cause the notice of the certification to be published in the District of Columbia Register.

(B) The date of publication of the notice of the certification shall not affect the applicability of this chapter.

(b) Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to affect any public-private partnerships, projects, or agreements that are developed, solicited, awarded, or entered into before this chapter applies.
