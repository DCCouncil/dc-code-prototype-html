Legislative history of Law 20-76

Law 20-76, the “Parent and Student Empowerment Amendment Act of 2013,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 20-314. The Bill was adopted on first and second readings on November 5, 2013 and December 3, 2013, respectively. Signed by the Mayor on December 16, 2013, it was assigned Act No. 20-242 and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 20-76 became effective on February 22, 2014.
