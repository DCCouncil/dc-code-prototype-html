Legislative history of Law 10-138

Law 10-138, the “Limited Liability Company Act of 1994,” was introduced in Council and assigned Bill No. 10-277, which was referred to the Committee on Consumer and Regulatory Affairs. Signed by the Mayor on May 18, 1994, it was assigned Act No. 10-243 and transmitted to both Houses of Congress for its review. D.C. Law 10-138 became effective on July 23, 1994.
